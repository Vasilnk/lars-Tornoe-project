Clover

Designed by Lars Tornøe | produced by Cooee Design

Clover is a simplified interpretation of classical porcelain styles that might bring
items from grandmothers kitchen to mind. Arranged in a triplet, the curved surfaces
generate a design language that is both botanical and geometric. The ceramic
vases are available in four sizes.
